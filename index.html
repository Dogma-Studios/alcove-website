Why Alcove

Community tools shouldn't all look the same

Discord is built for gamers. Slack is built for offices. Facebook Groups are built for algorithms. None of them are built for your community.

Alcove uses earthy tones, thoughtful spacing, and a distraction-free layout — because the space you gather in shapes how you connect. Cold tools make cold communities.

Built with Tauri — not Electron — and powered by a Rust backend. That means native speed, tiny footprint, and real-time messaging that doesn't skip a beat.

"The structure of a big platform. The warmth of a small one."
